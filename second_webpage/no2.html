Either for your good

Or theirs

What stays is

Part(s) of them

Either good ones

Or bad ones

Or sometimes both

No mattter what it's type is

But leave their parts with you

Sometimes intentionally

Sometimes unintentionally

And leaving you confused about

What to do with it

Find happiness

Because you still have it

At least a part of them

Or find sorrows

Because you can't have all of them

Whatever you choose

You come at war with yourself

And that forces you to break apart

Isn't it

And what stays

After every downhearted day

Is you

With or without their parts

So make sure it doesn't change

You into irreparable you

Rather helps in finding

Unfiltered you

Dauntless you

YOU you
